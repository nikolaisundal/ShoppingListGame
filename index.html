Welcome to my fun, fun game which I have created.. this is how you play!

1. Add groceries to the input "", press the add button "" for each item. Your list will appear below.


2. Memorize the list to the best of your ability and press the show/hide all button "".


3. Go to your supermarket of choice and find a couple of items, enter them in the input again, except this time press the attempt button "". The found items will appear at the top of you list! Keep doing this until you have found all of your items.


4. If you have trouble remembering an item you can press the hint button "".


5. If you want to show/hide an individual item press the mask button "". To remove an item press the trash button "".


6. When you are done you can reset your list by pressing the reset button "".


That's it, good luck!
